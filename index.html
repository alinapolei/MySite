    Q1: What is the difference between web1, web2, web3?

    1. In web1, unlike web2 and web3, there is no opportunity for users to share content like posting reviews, comments, and feedbacks. Web1 considered as a read only web.
    2. The web engines in each of the webs is critically different. In web1 the search based only on the URL of the site, web2 added the real search engine that was searched by site's tags which were added manually and web3 brought the semantic search by automatic tagged sites.
    3. Web3 added the interaction between machine devices and the internet, the field of IOT.

    Q2: A use case that describes the diferences

    For example the user want to search side effects of Advil tablets:
    In web1 the user needs to know the specific urls that talks about side effects of medicines, and check what is the side effects of Advil tablets.
    In web2 the user will google "side effects of Advil tablets", and he will get links to sites that are tagged with tags like Advil and side effects.
    In web3 the user will google "side effects of Advil tablets", and he will get links to sites, blogs or forums that talk or contain someone reviews about side effects of Advil tablets.
